The design process brings humanity closer to one another. Therefore, I contribute my time to designing software intended to improve peoples lives. My current obsession is identifying, measuring, and alleviating peoples frustrations from their digital experiences. What sets me apart from my peers is my relentless desire to go and talk to people in their personal context and my eye for how simple interactions with software can evoke uncommon emotions. The last thing you should know about me is I spend an incredible amount of time chasing down the curiousity I derive from my work.

Outside of work, I have a passion for running, spatial computing, endurance motorsports, esports, premium consumer hardware, and visual storytelling. My favorite book is "The Way Things Work" and my favorite movie is "Snatch". Below you will find a selection of work that I am proud to have been a part of.
